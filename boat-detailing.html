Conditions & Limitations

An Honest Note On Results

We work to deliver the best possible result on every boat — but there are limits to what a single service can resolve. We tell you up front, on arrival, what's realistic for your boat.

Permanent stains may persist

Some vinyl, carpet, and cushion stains have set in and won't fully release, even with steam cleaning and extraction.

Mildew & odors may need multiple passes

Mildew, mold, and musty odors that have absorbed into soft surfaces may require repeat treatment or specialized equipment to fully address.

Heavy oxidation is a restoration job

A detail won't remove heavy gel-coat oxidation or deep sun fading. Where restoration is possible, we'll tell you on arrival and quote it separately.

Everything assessed on arrival

Before any work begins, we walk the boat with you, photograph the starting condition, and confirm scope, timeline, and any condition upcharges.

Photos are taken before and after every service as standard practice — for your records and ours.
