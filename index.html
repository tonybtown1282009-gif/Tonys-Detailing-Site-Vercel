Conditions & Limitations

An Honest Note On Results

We take pride in delivering the best possible result on every vehicle — but there are limits to what a single service can resolve. We tell you up front, on arrival, what's realistic.

  • Permanent stains may persist

    Some carpet, headliner, and upholstery stains have set into the fibers and won't fully release with standard interior treatment.

  • Rust & oxidation can't be undone

    Rust, deep paint oxidation, and salt damage are structural — they require body work, not detailing.

  • Deep odors take multiple passes

    Smoke, mildew, and biological odors that have absorbed into seats and carpet may require repeat treatment or specialized equipment.

  • All conditions assessed on arrival

    Before any work begins, we walk the vehicle with you, photograph the starting condition, and confirm scope and any condition upcharges.

Photos are taken before and after every service as standard practice — for your records and ours.
