Common Questions

Good To Know

Tap any question to expand the answer.

How long does a detail take?
Most cars and SUVs take about 2–5 hours depending on the service and condition. An Exterior or Interior Detail is on the shorter end; a Full Detail or Deep Clean takes longer. Larger jobs like RVs and boats can run a full day or two. We give you a time estimate when we confirm your booking.
Do you need access to water and power?
Yes. We need access to a standard electrical outlet, and depending on the job we may also need an outdoor water spigot. If you're unsure what you have available, let us know when you book and we can plan around it.
What payment methods do you accept?
We currently accept cash only. Payment is due after the detail is finished and you're happy with the result.
What's your weather policy?
Because we're mobile, weather matters. Light conditions are usually fine, but heavy rain, storms, or extreme cold can affect quality, so we may reschedule to protect the result. If we need to move your appointment we'll reach out as early as possible to find a new time.
What areas do you serve?
We're based in Chardon and serve Geauga and Lake County plus the premium east-side Cleveland suburbs — including Chesterland, Chagrin Falls, Gates Mills, Kirtland, Willoughby, Mentor, and Concord. Farther out is possible by quote. If you're not sure you're in range, just ask.
Why might there be an upcharge?
Base prices assume normal wear. Heavy pet hair, deep staining, smoke or odor, and excessive debris take extra time and materials, so they can add to the price. Any upcharge is estimated and confirmed with you on arrival, before any work begins — no surprises at the end.
Do I need to be home, and where do you detail?
You don't have to be home the whole time, but we do need access to the vehicle and a spot to work — a driveway, parking area, or garage. We come to your home or office and set up on site.
How far in advance should I book?
A few days ahead is ideal, especially on weekends, but we'll always try to fit you in sooner when the schedule allows. Book online or call and we'll confirm within 24 hours. Please give at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule.
